About 万屋 YOROZUYA

Inspired by the hit anime Gintama, we adopted the 万事屋 (Yorozuya)—the “house of myriad jobs”—and made it real. 万 (yorozu) literally means “10,000,” but more importantly “everything under the sky.” At Yorozuya, we do any job (for a fair fee) and sell anything you can imagine.

Founded in 20XX by a odd-job enthusiasts, who started out fetching groceries, repairing gadgets, and sourcing rare anime merchandise. Word spread: whether you need event staffing at the last minute, a custom cosplay prop, or a simple errand run, you bring the request—we bring the results.

Our name, 万屋, isn’t just branding; it’s a promise: “You need it done, we’ll do it—and you need it found, we’ll find it.”

Explore our site to browse the latest products, join our rewards program, or drop us a wild request—because at 万屋 YOROZUYA, truly anything under the sky is just a click away.
